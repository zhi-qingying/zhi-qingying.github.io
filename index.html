📊 Key Insights from SQL Analysis

  • Price Ranking: Central & Western district has highest avg price per sqft (HKD $87,200+), followed by Wan Chai and Southern
  • Price-Area Correlation: Negative correlation (-0.42) confirms smaller units have higher price per sqft
  • Most Active Estate: THE VICTORIA recorded 180+ transactions across all districts
  • New Sale Premium: New Sale transactions average 8-12% higher price per sqft than Resale
